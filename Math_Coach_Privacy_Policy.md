
# Privacy Policy for Math Coach

**Last updated: February 19, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Math Coach ("the App") is an educational mathematics application designed for children aged 10+ and families.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

## 2. Data Collection

Math Coach:

- Does NOT collect personal data
- Does NOT collect email addresses
- Does NOT collect names
- Does NOT collect age information
- Does NOT collect location data
- Does NOT collect device identifiers
- Does NOT use analytics services
- Does NOT use advertising services
- Does NOT track users

The App works fully offline and does not transmit any data to external servers.

## 3. Accounts

The App does not require user registration.
The App does not support login or account creation.

## 4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer a one-time Pro upgrade through Google Play Billing.

All payments are processed securely by Google Play.  
The developer does not receive or store any payment information such as credit card details.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Math Coach is intended for users aged 10 and older and is suitable for family use.

Since the App does not collect any personal data, it complies with applicable children's privacy standards, including Google Play Families Policy requirements.

## 6. Data Storage

All progress and learning data are stored locally on the user’s device only.
No data is transmitted externally.

If the app is uninstalled, all locally stored data may be removed.

## 7. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external tracking tools.

The only third-party service involved is Google Play for app distribution and optional in-app purchases.

## 8. Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit data, there is no external data storage or processing performed by the developer.
